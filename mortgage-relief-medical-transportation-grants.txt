Mortgage Relief and Medical Transportation Grants
===============================================

1. Mortgage Relief: Homeowner Assistance Fund (HAF)
   - Description: Federal program providing financial assistance to homeowners for mortgage payments, property taxes, and utilities.
   - Eligibility: Homeowners experiencing financial hardship due to COVID-19. Administered by states.
   - Link: https://www.ncsha.org/homeowner-assistance-fund/

2. Medical Transportation: Medi-Cal Non-Emergency Medical Transportation (NEMT)
   - Description: California's Medi-Cal program covers non-emergency medical transportation for eligible recipients.
   - Eligibility: Medi-Cal recipients in California who need transportation to medical appointments.
   - Link: https://www.dhcs.ca.gov/services/medi-cal/Pages/Transportation.aspx
